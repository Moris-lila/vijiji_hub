About Us

Welcome to Vijiji Hub, where transformative entrepreneurship flourishes from urban slums to remote villages. We're a catalyst for inclusive innovation, fostering sustainable businesses through collaboration, mentorship, and community empowerment. At Vijiji Hub, we believe in the power of visionary leaders to drive positive change and redefine the future

Main Focus

From urban slums to remote villages (rural areas), Vijiji Hub is driving inclusive and impact- driven model to sustainable innovative entrepreneurship. Our main goal is to support start-ups! From urban slums to remote villages, we identify the unseen innovators and link them to potential investors, donors, organizations/mentors and networks. We endeavour to bring incubation and business support close to our youth/community to ensure their ideas thrive, develop skills and networks and give these unemployed and forgotten groups a chance to participate and be seen by the world.
